# Summary

A ball and socket mount for the Prusa MK2 bed and Raspberry Pi camera. The case allows for the use of a fisheye lens to get a full view of the printbed.

Parts:
- Raspberry Pi Camera
- Longer Ribbon Cable for Camera. This will depend on where you plan to mount your server. I used 75cm.
- Fisheye Lens (optional)
- M3x18mm screw (for attaching the cam to the ball joint)
- M3x10mm screw (for attaching the mount to the y carriage)
- M3 nut, no more than 2.5mm thick
