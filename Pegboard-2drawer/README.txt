# Summary

The next in my pegboard series - I have lots of electronic components and assorted small parts scattered around my workbench.  This small organizer bin has separate mounting pins to make printing easier and eliminate the need for supports.  The drawer has a slot for a 1/2"  x 2" piece of paper so you can label the drawer contents.  You will need 1 print of the bin, 2 prints of the drawer and 4 prints of the mounting pins.  I printed this at 0.3 resolution - doesn't really need anything finer.
